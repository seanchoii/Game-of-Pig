This is a game of Pig!

Each player takes turns rolling a single die and
the roll results are added to the score.

Each player takes turns rolling a single die
and the roll results are added to the score

- If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing
and it becomes next player's turn

- If the player rolls another number, it is added
to their turn total and the player's turn continues

- If the player decides to hold, their turn is over
and their turn total is added to the total score

This game continues this way until a player reaches
100 total points. Then the game is over.
